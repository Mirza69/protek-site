Work Model

Cybersecurity Analyst Tiers

Structured career progression with clearly defined responsibilities at each level

1

Tier 1

Entry-Level SOC Analyst

  • Monitor SIEM and EDR platforms
  • Triage and escalate alerts
  • Support initial containment
  • Maintain case documentation
  • Basic log analysis
2

Tier 2

Intermediate Analyst

  • In-depth incident investigations
  • Root cause analysis
  • Vulnerability and malware analysis
  • Threat intelligence correlation
  • Detection rule optimization
3

Tier 3

Senior SOC Analyst

  • Lead complex investigations
  • Threat hunting (MITRE ATT&CK)
  • Detection use case development
  • Mentor junior analysts
  • Incident reporting

What You'll Bring

  • 1–5+ years' experience in a SOC or cybersecurity operations role (aligned to Tier)
  • Familiarity with SIEM platforms, EDR tools, vulnerability scanners, and network monitoring
  • Working knowledge of ISO 27001, NIST CSF, MITRE ATT&CK, and relevant frameworks
  • Strong analytical skills and curiosity to dig into logs, alerts, and threat behaviours
  • Ability to explain findings clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • A team mindset with a passion for learning, mentoring, and staying ahead of threats

Bonus Skills

  • Experience with Microsoft 365 Defender, Sentinel, Splunk, or CrowdStrike
  • Prior background in IT support, systems administration, or networking
  • Certifications such as CompTIA Security+, GSEC, Microsoft SC-200, or similar

  • Access structured training, paid certs, and clear promotion paths
  • Join a collaborative, consultancy-first culture where you're trusted and empowered
  • Enjoy flexible working, bonuses, private medical, and team retreats
