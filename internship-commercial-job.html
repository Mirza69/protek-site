Internship – Commercial Track

Hybrid (UK-based)
6–12 Months
Apply Now

Overview

ProtekCyber’s Commercial Internship is perfect for those interested in the business side of cybersecurity. You’ll work with our sales, marketing, and bid teams to learn how security services are designed, priced, and delivered to clients. This role bridges technical knowledge with commercial strategy—ideal for future cybersecurity consultants or sales engineers.

£20k
Annual Stipend
1:1
Mentorship
30%
Client-Facing Time

What You’ll Do

  • Assist in creating proposals, pitch decks, and service descriptions.
  • Shadow client meetings and contribute to security requirement gathering.
  • Research procurement frameworks (G-Cloud, DOS, Cyber Essentials).
  • Analyze competitor offerings and market trends.
  • Help design client onboarding materials and security awareness content.
  • Support marketing campaigns and event planning.

Who You Are

  • Studying Business, Marketing, or a technical field with commercial interest.
  • Strong written/verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to translate technical concepts for non-technical audiences.
  • Familiarity with CRM tools (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce) is a plus.
  • Interest in cybersecurity compliance (ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR).

Perks

  • £20,000 annual stipend + performance bonuses.
  • Industry certification (e.g., BCS Foundation, APMP).
  • Networking opportunities with clients and partners.
  • Pathway to Bid Writer or Security Consultant roles.
