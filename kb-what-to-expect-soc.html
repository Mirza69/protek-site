SOC Operations Process

Our proven workflow ensures comprehensive protection and rapid response to security incidents.

1

Data Collection & Normalization

We ingest and normalize security data from your endpoints, networks, cloud environments, and applications. Our platform supports 500+ integrations with security tools and platforms.

2

Continuous Monitoring & Detection

Our security analysts and AI systems monitor your environment 24/7/365, using correlation rules, behavioral analytics, and threat intelligence to identify potential security incidents.

3

Alert Triage & Investigation

Each alert is evaluated by our Tier 1 analysts, with escalation to Tier 2/3 for complex threats. We provide enriched context and risk assessment for every incident.

4

Incident Response

For confirmed incidents, our team follows playbook-driven response procedures, including containment, eradication, and recovery guidance tailored to your environment.

5

Reporting & Improvement

Regular reports detail threats detected, response actions, and security posture improvements. We continuously refine detection rules based on new intelligence.
