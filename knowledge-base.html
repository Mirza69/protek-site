Frequently Asked Questions

What is SOC as a Service and how does it benefit my business?

▼

SOC as a Service (Security Operations Center as a Service) provides 24/7 monitoring, threat detection, and incident response capabilities without the need to build and staff your own security operations center. This service benefits your business by providing enterprise-grade security expertise and technology at a predictable cost, reducing the time to detect and respond to threats, and allowing your internal IT team to focus on strategic initiatives rather than constant security monitoring.

How often should we conduct penetration testing?

▼

The frequency of penetration testing depends on several factors including your industry, regulatory requirements, and rate of change in your IT environment. Generally, we recommend conducting comprehensive penetration tests at least annually, with additional testing after significant infrastructure changes, major application updates, or when new regulations affect your security requirements. For organizations in highly regulated industries or those with sensitive data, quarterly or bi-annual testing may be more appropriate.

What compliance standards does ProtekCyber help with?

▼

ProtekCyber provides comprehensive support for numerous compliance standards including GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST CSF, CCPA/CPRA, and industry-specific regulations. Our team of compliance experts works with your organization to understand your specific regulatory requirements, implement necessary controls, prepare for audits, and maintain ongoing compliance through continuous monitoring and regular assessments.

How quickly can you respond to a security incident?

▼

Our incident response capabilities operate 24/7/365, with critical alerts receiving response within 60 minutes. Our Service Level Agreement (SLA) defines different response times based on the severity of the incident: Critical incidents receive immediate attention with a 1-hour response time, High severity incidents within 2 hours, and Medium severity incidents within 3 hours. Our continuous monitoring ensures rapid detection, and our experienced incident response team follows established procedures to contain, eradicate, and recover from security incidents with minimal business disruption.
