Key Compliance Frameworks

We specialize in these critical regulatory standards affecting UK businesses.

GDPR

The General Data Protection Regulation governs how organizations process personal data of EU/UK citizens.

Key Requirements:

Data Mapping DPO Appointment Breach Notification Privacy by Design
Maximum fine: £17.5M or 4% global turnover

NIS Regulations

Network and Information Systems regulations secure UK essential services and digital providers.

Applies To:

Mandatory breach reporting within 72 hours
